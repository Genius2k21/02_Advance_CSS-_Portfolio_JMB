About Me

Hello! My name is Joseph Blake and I am a student of Georgia Tech's Coding Bootcamp. This is my portfolio showcasing my developing talent and skills. I am a United States Air Force Veteran with six years of Active Duty Service as a Cyber Systems Operator. I have served in both Iraq and Afghanistan. During my enlistment I earned both my Associates in Applied Science in Information Systems Technology from the Community College of the Air Force and my Bachelors of Applied Science in Adult Education. Following my military service I continued my education achieving my Master's in Computer Information Systems. Currently, I work as a Claims Adjuster for GEICO. I am seeking to enhance my talent and skill set to ultimately transition into an Information Technology role where I can apply my extensive knowledge, skill, and ability to enhance an organization performance objectives.
